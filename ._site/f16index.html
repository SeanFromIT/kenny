UFO Incident of July 26, 2002
On July 26, 2002, NORAD acknowledged the radar detectionof an unidentified flying object approaching the restricted flight zonearound Washington D.C. Two Air National Guard jets were scrambled fromAndrews Air Force Base as the mysterious target 'faded' from radar. Inthe same time frame, residents from nearby Waldorf Maryland report seeingjets chase a mysterious light in the sky.
