Ohio River Hotspot?
BY KENNY YOUNG

On February 2, 2002, an anonymous person from Maysville, Kentuckyfiled an on-line UFO report complaining of a strange aircraft seen overthe Ohio river between the power plants. An independent investigation ofthat claim by Yours Truly revealed that a security guard at one of theplants along the river near Maysville, Kentucky had logged the sightingof an unusual aircraft in his logbook at the same date and time. Despitethis strong authentication of the sighting report, local Maysville, Kentuckynews media would not assist in further exploration of this subject.

In the following months, a number of other similar reports beganto surface from the Ohio River city of Portsmouth. A Portsmouth residentinformed that he would spy an unusual object in the late night hours, oftencoming down in the vicinity of the bridges. He would usually retreat toa high position on top of a Portsmouth church building to view the episode.In response to this and several other reports from this individual, a "UFOWatch" was undertaken in the summer of 2002 with the Portsmouth residentand another participant from West Portsmouth, Ohio, plus Yours Truly.

Lexington, Kentucky resident Earle Benezet of Kentucky MUFON advisedthat several fisherman from Covington, Kentucky have also alerted him toUFO activity along the Ohio River near New Richmond, Ohio. The witness,reportedly along with other numerous fishermen, Benezet said, have reportedcertain and large triangular objects, one said to be 'a football field'in length. He advised that one reported object obstructed starglow. Onefisherman offered to accompany Benezet to the location but that activityis pending as Benezet has yet been able to obtain some kind of follow upto the reports.

And as of May 1, 2003, another report of a UFO spying the southernOhio River area between Cincinnati and Huntington, WVA has crept up, thisreport originating from Ashland, Kentucky, as reported to the NationalUFO Center in Seattle, Washington. That report is enclosed below.

There is a similarity in the reported objects recently seen alongthe Ohio River, many described as illuminated triangular objects the sizeof a small car. What is going on along the Ohio River between Cincinnatiand Ashland, Kentucky? Has this area become a new UFO hotspot?
