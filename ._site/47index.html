1947 UFO CRASHES

This page is a general index of reported UFO crashes from the summer of 1947. The focus of this database is weighted with physical cases, not abounding with "anonymous insider" stories.

The links provided will take you to more information about the specific case. Information is arranged in chronological order. Beneath this is another database arranged by category of crash report [such as cases involving fire, smoke, heat, light, etc.], followed by a listing of crash reports 'by state.'

If you have information that could be added to this database, please send E-mail to Kenny Young.
